🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024  |  « Back to Home

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Find My Match (findmymatch.ebookdesire.com) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our matchmaking service. The types of information we collect include:

Type of Data Examples Purpose
Account Information Name, email address, date of birth, password Account creation and authentication
Profile Information Gender, location, photos, bio, interests, relationship goals Displaying your profile and matching you with others
Usage Data Pages visited, features used, login times, search queries Improving service quality and user experience
Communications Messages sent between users, support tickets Facilitating connections and resolving issues
Payment Information Billing address, transaction ID (not card numbers) Processing premium subscriptions
Device & Technical Data IP address, browser type, device type, cookies Security, fraud prevention, analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Suggest compatible matches based on your profile and preferences.
  • Enable communication between members.
  • Send you service-related notifications and updates.
  • Process payments for premium memberships.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, spam, and abuse.
  • Improve our algorithms, features, and overall user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We will never sell your personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. How We Share Your Information

Find My Match does not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users: Information you choose to display on your public profile is visible to other registered members.
  • Service providers: We work with trusted third-party vendors (e.g., payment processors, email services) who are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred as part of the business assets.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on Find My Match. Cookies help us remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how users interact with the service.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our platform.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (login sessions, security).
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users navigate the site (e.g., Google Analytics).

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously. We implement industry-standard measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL) for all data transmission.
  • Hashed and salted password storage.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.

Important: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong, unique password for your account.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you delete your account, we will remove your personally identifiable information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, legal disputes).

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile settings.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email we send.
  • Data portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Restrict processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@findmymatch.ebookdesire.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Find My Match is strictly for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user under 18 has created an account, we will immediately terminate the account and delete all associated data. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will notify you via email or a notice on our website. Your continued use of Find My Match after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Website: findmymatch.ebookdesire.com
  • Email: privacy@findmymatch.ebookdesire.com
