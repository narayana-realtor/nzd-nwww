❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Find My Match free to use?

Yes! Basic membership is completely free. You can browse profiles, receive messages, and create your account at no cost. Premium features like unlimited messaging and advanced filters are available with a paid plan.

How do I report a suspicious profile?

Click the "Report" button on any profile or message. Our Trust & Safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours. You can also email safety@findmymatch.ebookdesire.com directly.

How are profiles verified?

We use a combination of email verification, phone verification, and AI-powered photo checks to ensure profiles are genuine. Look for the blue verification tick on profiles.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days as per our Privacy Policy.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?

Go to Settings > Subscription and click "Cancel Plan". You'll continue to have premium access until the end of your billing period.
