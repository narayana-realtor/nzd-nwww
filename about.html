Whether you're looking for your soulmate, a life partner, or simply a genuine friend, Find My Match is here to guide your journey — one heartbeat at a time.

Our Story

Find My Match was born in 2019 when our founders Aryan and Sunita — both engineers with a passion for human connection — noticed a gap in India's online dating landscape. Most apps focused on casual swiping, while millions of young professionals across India were searching for something deeper and more meaningful.

They built Find My Match around a simple philosophy: show people who you truly are, and you will find who truly matches you. The platform launched in Mumbai with 500 members, and within a year had grown to 50,000 members across 20 cities.

Today, with over half a million active members and thousands of success stories, Find My Match continues to be India's most trusted relationship platform — because we believe every heart deserves to find its perfect match.
